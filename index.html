about

I'm a tech founder and operator in AI / climate. I've been turning as a hobby for twenty years — my first lathe was a barmitzvah gift from my grandfather. I now work in the micro-studio I designed and built in my garden in Tottenham. My favourite pieces are simple, tactile, and built around imperfections in the wood: cracks, burrs, bark, water stains.
