about

I'm a tech founder based in London. I've been turning as a hobby for twenty years — my first lathe was a barmitzvah gift from my grandfather. I now work in the micro-studio I designed and built in our garden in Tottenham. My favourite pieces are simple, tactile, and built around imperfections in the wood: cracks, spiralling grain, water stains. As a Rabbi's son, I've also made judaica over the years: mezuzot, etz chaim for Torah scrolls, seder plates.
